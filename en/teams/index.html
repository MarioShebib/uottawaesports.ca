Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO)

CS:GO is the perfect game for both high levels of competition, as well as fun times with friends. We like to have a good balance between both values, so you will find a mix of competitive events, such as 5v5 and 2v2 in-house tournaments, as well as fun times with friends, such as minigame nights. There are also lots of giveaways and prized events so you might even get to fund your coffee addiction.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a free-to-play, 5v5 team-based RPG. Each player plays a position in which the 5 players work together to attack the opponents base. We have 3 competitive teams (Collegiate, Academy, Rookie) at uOttawa Esports and over 125 players in our Discord. League of Legends is the game for you if you want to meet some great people and get away from the books!

Rainbow Six Siege

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical 5v5 first-person shooter developed by Ubisoft. We offer both a competitive and casual side of the siege community. We currently have 2 competitive teams and good sized community of players who enjoy a more casual aspect of the game. If you want a game to play with some friends and get away from the books Rainbow Six Siege is for you!

Rocket League

Rocket League is a physics-based car soccer game with the winning objective being to score more goals than the other team. Each player has their own car and attempts to maneuver the ball into the opposing team's net within five minutes of thrilling, high-octane gameplay! We host tons of events ranging from giveaways, tournaments and contests of all shapes and sizes! We also share a wide range of opportunities on the competitive side, from Psyonix's CRL, to CSL, to OPSE, to AVGL, and to PlayVS. Our mantra is to give any willing student the chance to play regardless of skill level, so make sure to come by and say hi if you're interested!

VALORANT

VALORANT is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows. We offer both a competitive and casual side where we host inhouse tournaments with awesome prizes! Bring your friends, form a team and have fun!
