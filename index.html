About Us

Welcome to uOttawa Esports, your home for Esports on and off of campus. Join us for both casual and competitive gaming at the University of Ottawa! The competitive teams that we are currently fielding are listed with their respective contact(s). Join our discord server and say hi to our amazing community!

A Community for All

Not only do we offer a competitive environment for the more competitive gamer, but also a friendly and safe environment for anyone wanting to pick up a new game, to find players to play with, or even to get away from the books!
